Please fill out this form to request a centrally organised Carpentries workshop. We will ask you about dates, location and workshop type, so please plan to have this information available. Please be advised that a workshop will not be scheduled until it has been confirmed by the host and The Carpentries Workshop Administrator. Our workshops are organised based on the order the Workshop Request Form is received and finalized.

If you would like more information about our workshops before scheduling, please request workshop information. If you are a Member Organisation or Instructor who is coordinating a self-organised workshop please use this form to register your workshop.
